ComboCodex is a website where users can share and view "team combos" from the platform fighting game Brawlhalla. Though the target audience is relatively niche, the site averages around 25,000 requests daily, with nearly 1,000 registered users and over 10,000 total uploaded videos, taking up around 50GB of storage. While ComboCodex wasn't my first project, it was the first I completed and hosted. Originally launched in February 2023, the site was completely remade in July 2023 and received a UI overhaul in December 2023. I am currently in the process of remaking the website again to reflect my growth as a developer.

The site also features a small API used to integrate with a Discord bot. ComboCodex originally began as a Discord server, which remains active today, and many members still use the bot to interact with the website.
