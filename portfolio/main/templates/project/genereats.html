Genereats is a website where visitors can create and search for AI-generated recipes. Compared to my other projects, Genereats was more of a learning experience and a "fun" project. It gave me the opportunity to integrate AI (specifically the OpenAI API) into my work and experiment with creating more interactive and visually appealing UIs using Hyperscript. Due to its nature, Genereats doesn’t get many visitors, and I don’t have immediate plans for further updates. As such, it will likely remain a "complete" project for the time being.
