Genereats is a website where visitors can create and search recipes generated by AI. Compared to my other projects, Genereats was much more of a learning experience and "fun" project. I used Genereats as an opporunity to learn how to integrate AI (speicifcally the OpenAI API) into my project, and create more interactive and pleasing UIs (via Hyperscript). Due to the nature of the project Genereats does not get many visitors, and I do not have many plans at all to update it in the future. As such it will likely remain a "complete" project for the time being.
