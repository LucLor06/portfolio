Hyperscript is an awesome library (made by the same creators as HTMX) that makes attaching event listeners and behaviors to elements effortless, thanks to its intuitive and easy-to-read syntax. Hyperscript has allowed me to create not only more interactive UIs but also significantly faster ones. While it’s not as widely known as HTMX, I’ve found it incredibly useful in a variety of scenarios, breathing life into my front-end projects.
